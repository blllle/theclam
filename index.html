About the Clam

Once upon a time, 3 adult alcohol enthusiasts ( or enthusiastic alcoholic adults ) regularly mixed cocktails at home at the kitchen bench. That all changed one summer afternoon when they stumbled across an old school bar at a local Salvos. Too good an opportunity to pass up, they purchased it immediately and it was delivered the next morning to a back street in the heart of Fitzroy. At first it seemed a ridiculous purchase as they rearranged the furniture to make way for the new addition, but it was a no brainer - 1 of 5 couches was given the flick, a bottle of prosecco was popped and The Thirsty Clam was born at 10:10am on new years eve 2017...
